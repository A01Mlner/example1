About This Training

Join us every Wednesday for an exciting journey into the world of Git! Our sessions are designed for everyone—from beginners to advanced users—looking to enhance their version control skills. Each week, we cover a different aspect of Git, including:

  • Basic Commands (Clone, Add, Commit)
  • Branching and Merging
  • Resolving Conflicts
  • Collaboration Workflows
  • Using GitHub and GitLab

Whether you’re working on personal projects or collaborating with a team, mastering Git is essential for efficient development!
